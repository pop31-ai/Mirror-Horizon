# Neon Drift Racing

## Core Gameplay Loop
Race through procedurally generated neon tracks, drifting through corners to build boost, collecting energy orbs, and battling rivals across multiple laps.

## Unique Mechanic
Drift-boost system: the longer and more precise your drift through corners, the more boost energy you accumulate. Perfect drifts through apex zones grant massive speed bursts.

## Brief Description
In a cyberpunk megacity where street racing is the ultimate underground sport, you pilot a hover-racer through luminescent tracks that weave between skyscrapers, through neon-lit tunnels, and across holographic bridges. Each race is a test of precision drifting, strategic boost management, and split-second navigation through dynamically shifting track segments.

## Game Concept Overview

**Setting**: Neo-Tokyo 2087, a sprawling cyberpunk metropolis where hover-racing dominates the underground circuit. Tracks run through district sectors — from the rain-soaked neon markets of Sector 7 to the sterile white corridors of the Corporate Spire.

**Player Class**: Drift Pilot — Specialists in high-speed cornering and boost management.

**Core Mechanics**:
- **Drift System**: Initiate drifts by holding shift + direction; longer drifts = more boost meter
- **Boost Zones**: Glowing apex markers on corners that multiply drift energy when hit precisely
- **Energy Orbs**: Scattered along tracks for small speed pickups and shield restoration
- **Hover Racer**: Physics-based hover handling with momentum and air control
- **Track Hazards**: Oil slicks, EMP zones, and rival interference

**Track Generation**:
- Procedurally assembled from modular track segments (straights, curves, S-bends, ramps)
- Each district has unique visual themes and hazard types
- Weather and time-of-day affect track conditions and visibility
- Ghost data from previous runs provides competitive targets

**Progression System**:
- Unlock new hover-racer chassis with different handling profiles
- Upgrade boost capacity, drift sensitivity, and top speed
- Earn neon customizations (underglow, trail effects, wing patterns)
- Access hidden shortcut routes through mastery of advanced drift techniques

**Rival System**:
- AI rivals with distinct personalities (aggressive, defensive, technical)
- Rival drifters can block, boost-check, and draft behind you
- Defeating rivals unlocks their signature move or modification

**Narrative Elements**:
- Underground racing circuit with escalating stakes
- Corporate sponsorship deals with hidden agendas
- Rivalries that evolve through race outcomes
- The mystery of the "Phantom Drifter" who races in every district

**Technical Challenges**:
- Smooth hover physics with momentum-based handling
- Procedural track generation that maintains raceability
- Visual flair with bloom, trails, and particle effects at high speed
- Networked ghost data for asynchronous competition

**Potential Expansions**:
- Team-based relay races across district boundaries
- Track editor for community-created circuits
- Seasonal tournament circuits with exclusive rewards
- Hover-racer crafting and tuning workshop

# Ecology Paradox

## Core Gameplay Loop
Modify ecosystem parameters to achieve specific outcomes in a post-apocalyptic landscape.

## Unique Mechanic
Dynamic ecosystem simulator where predator-prey ratios, climate zones, and biodiversity drive emergent gameplay.

## Brief Description
In a world where human intervention is forbidden, you must carefully balance ecosystem restoration in a former industrial wasteland. Every decision shapes biomes, wildlife behavior, and human settlements that may or may not return.

## Game Concept Overview

**Setting**: The Rust Belt - abandoned industrial zones where nature is slowly reclaiming human structures.

**Player Role**: Ecosystem Balancer - Scientist working to restore ecological harmony.

**Core Mechanics**:
- **Species Introduction**: Bring back extinct or endangered species
- **Climate Control**: Manipulate weather patterns and temperature zones
- **Biome Engineering**: Transform landscapes between different ecosystem types
- **Succession Management**: Guide ecological succession through various stages

**Technical Implementation**:
- **Dynamic Weather System**: Real-time climate simulation
- **Food Web Modeling**: Complex predator-prey and symbiotic relationships
- **Mutation System**: Genetic evolution and adaptation mechanisms
- **Settlement Dynamics**: Human reintegration based on ecosystem health

**Key Systems**:
1. **Wildlife Sanctuaries**: Protected areas for species recovery
2. **Research Laboratories**: Genetic modification and breeding programs
3. **Monitoring Stations**: Track ecosystem indicators
4. **Human Camps**: Settlements that can adapt or be destroyed

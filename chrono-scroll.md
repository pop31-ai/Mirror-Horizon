# Chrono-Scroll

## Core Gameplay Loop
Collect time fragments while navigating through shifting timelines where past events influence the present.

## Unique Mechanic
Non-linear timeline mechanics where you can manipulate events in the past to solve present challenges.

## Brief Description
In a modular city where history repeats like broken records, you play as a "Chrono-Cutter" who can rewind time snippets to extract crucial items, but each change creates ripple effects that alter the city's layout and available resources.

## Game Concept Overview

**Setting**: A dystopian city where time flows in cycles, creating variations of the same urban landscape.

**Player Class**: Chrono-Cutter - Specialists in temporal manipulation and extraction.

**Core Mechanics**:
- **Timeline Navigation**: Jump between different time periods of the same city location
- **Fragment Collection**: Gather temporal energy fragments left behind by timeline shifts
- **Ripple Effect System**: Each past alteration creates cascading changes to the present
- **Resource Management**: Balance time fragments against temporal stability

**Procedural Generation**:
- Each timeline generates unique architectural layouts
- Historical events create resource opportunities and hazards
- Weather patterns and city layouts dynamically shift

**Progression System**:
- Unlock new slicing techniques
- Upgrade Chrono-Cutter's temporal blade
- Access ancient timeline archives
- Master complex ripple effect patterns

**Narrative Elements**:
- Mystery of the repeating cycles
- Players discover fragmented histories
- Each timeline offers moral choices
- The city's evolution affects mission objectives

**Technical Challenges**:
- Implementing non-linear time mechanics
- Managing performance with multiple timeline states
- Creating emergent narrative from timeline combinations
- Balancing player power with game difficulty

**Potential Expansions**:
- Branching timelines based on player choices
- NPC interactions that affect timeline stability
- Time-traveling companions with unique abilities
- Ritual-based timeline manipulation

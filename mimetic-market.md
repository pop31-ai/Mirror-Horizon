# Mimetic Market

## Core Gameplay Loop
Trade and craft using skills borrowed from people you encounter.

## Unique Mechanic
Memory absorption where you can "copy" abilities by observing others' actions.

## Brief Description
Navigate a maze-like bazaar where every stall trader embodies a specific craft (carpentry, performance art, engineering). By watching their techniques and mentally replicating their movements, you unlock new recipes, building techniques, and social strategies for advancing in this community.

## Game Concept Overview

**Setting**: The Paradox Bazaar - a shifting marketplace where merchants are living libraries of forgotten crafts.

**Player Role**: Mimic Trader - Adaptive learner who can master any craft through observation.

**Core Mechanics**:
- **Observation Mode**: Study NPC actions to unlock new abilities
- **Skill Crafting**: Combine learned skills to create powerful items and technology
- **Social Negotiation**: Bargain with merchants using newly learned social techniques
- **Memory Storage**: Save and access skills in personal inventory

**System Design**:
- **Learning Mechanics**: Interactive watching system with skill capture
- **Craft Table**: Combine multiple skills for enhanced results
- **Merchant Districts**: Specialized areas for different types of crafts
- **Story Progress**: Each region teaches progressively more complex skills

**Skill Categories**:
1. **Material Trades**: Woodworking, metalworking, textiles
2. **Service Trades**: Performance, counseling, maintenance
3. **Technical Trades**: Engineering, alchemy, manufacturing
4. **Artistic Trades**: Painting, music, poetry

**Progression System**:
- Novice trader learns basic skills
- Intermediate trader masters complex combinations
- Master trader creates unique hybrid abilities
- Legendary trader invents revolutionary technologies
